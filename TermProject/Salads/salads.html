How to make the BEST Salad ON THE GO

Step One.

Buy yourself a good container. Its a good idea to get one that is airtight so it will not spill in your bag. Its advisable to get a container that has a spot for your salad dressing.

Step Two.

Take along disposable cutlery. You don't want extra washing after you come home. After you finish eating you can throw away the germs!

Step Three.

Building your salad. The first step in building your salad is to put in leafy greens. Examples of leafy green vegetables are: lettuce, spinach, kale, broccoli etc.

Step Four.

Put in ingredients to make your salad exciting! To ensure your salad is filling, put in 2-3 fruits or vegetables or pasta. Also, you can add protein (such as: fish, chicken, eggs, cheese...) to make it more filling.

Step Five.

Package it up and put it in your bag. Put it in a ziploc bag to be certain that you will not have any salad juice in your bag.

Step Six.

When its lunchtime, dig in! Enjoy! Tip: You can make it at night and store it in the fridge until the morning if its too hard to make it before you leave.
